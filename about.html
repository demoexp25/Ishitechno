About Us

An engineering studio built around the breadboard, not the brochure

Ishi Techno Projects is a multidisciplinary embedded systems and AI engineering team based in Andhra Pradesh, working with students, researchers, startups and industry across India.

Our Story

From single-board prototypes to industrial deployments

What started as hands-on Arduino and embedded consulting for engineering students has grown into a full-stack hardware and AI engineering practice — covering microcontroller firmware, PCB design, IoT connectivity, computer vision and industrial automation.

We work the same way whether the deliverable is a final-year B.Tech project or a production batch of IoT devices: clear specifications, tested hardware, and documentation that holds up under scrutiny.

M

Mission

Make production-grade embedded and AI engineering accessible — to a final-year student and an industrial client alike.

V

Vision

To become India's most trusted partner for applied embedded systems, IoT and AI prototyping.

P

Principles

Tested firmware. Honest timelines. Transparent pricing. No black-box deliverables.

How We Work

A disciplined four-stage delivery process

01

Discover

Requirements, constraints and success criteria, agreed in writing.

02

Design

Schematic, component selection and architecture review.

03

Build

Firmware, PCB and enclosure development with iterative testing.

04

Deliver

Final testing, documentation and handover or deployment.
