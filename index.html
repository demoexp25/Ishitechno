About Us

Engineering the bridge between an idea and a working device

Ishi Techno Projects is a technology innovation studio building embedded systems, IoT, AI and robotics solutions from first sketch to finished prototype. We work hands-on with microcontrollers, sensors, communication modules and custom PCBs — turning concepts into devices that actually run.

We support students preparing academic and final-year projects, researchers building working prototypes, and businesses and government teams that need a reliable hardware-and-software partner for automation, tracking and monitoring systems.
